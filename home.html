Who We Are

Udugu Ufanisi Ustawi wa Jamii (3UJ) Development Limited is a Woman-Led Kenyan microenterprise established in 2019. We empower communities through value-based actions towards sustainable human development and attainment of a lasting dignified healthy life for every individual. We strive to build dignified communities where we work and create social impact and local ownership of programs. We respect the dignity, sanctity of human life and rule of law.
