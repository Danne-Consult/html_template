About Us

We empower our communities to achieve sustainable development.

Udugu Ufanisi Ustawi wa Jamii (3UJ) Development Limited is a Kenyan organization founded on the essence of four words Udugu, Ufanisi ,Ustawi and Jamii. “Udugu” is our fraternal bond, “Ufanisi” is excellence, “Ustawi” is prosperity. and “Jamii”

Our mission is to empower our communities to achieve sustainable and lasting human development through value-based actions. We envision “Communities where every individual lives a sustainable healthy and dignified life”.

Our Values

3UJ is founded on five core values: Accountability, Integrity, Mutual Respect, Excellence and Social Inclusion. We are Accountable to our beneficiaries and customers. Integrity is at the core of everything we do. We are honest, fair and humble. Our Mutual respect and trust mean that we value and treat our people as equals and trustworthy individuals, each with different experience, skills and resources. Our approach to Social inclusion is to treat everyone respectfully; we listen and invite others to participate in everything we do.
